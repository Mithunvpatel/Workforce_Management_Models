# Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)

## Project Management
### 1.1 Project Planning
- 1.1.1 Define Project Scope
- 1.1.2 Develop Project Plan
- 1.1.3 Schedule Team Meetings

### 1.2 Risk Management
- 1.2.1 Identify Risks
- 1.2.2 Develop Risk Mitigation Plan
- 1.2.3 Implement Risk Controls

## Construction and Leasehold Improvements
### 2.1 Construction Completion
- 2.1.1 Finalize Construction Work
- 2.1.2 Inspect Construction Quality

### 2.2 Technical Improvements
- 2.2.1 Electrical Systems Testing
- 2.2.2 Plumbing Systems Testing
- 2.2.3 Security Features Testing

## Infrastructure and Equipment
### 3.1 IT Infrastructure
- 3.1.1 Purchase IT Equipment
- 3.1.2 Install IT Infrastructure

### 3.2 Telecommunications Equipment
- 3.2.1 Purchase Telecommunications Equipment
- 3.2.2 Install Telecommunications Equipment

## Coordination and Communication
### 4.1 Internal Coordination
- 4.1.1 Coordinate with AGM Staff
- 4.1.2 Schedule Staff Training

### 4.2 External Communication
- 4.2.1 Notify Clients of Address Change
- 4.2.2 Notify Suppliers of Address Change

## Physical Move
### 5.1 Move Preparation
- 5.1.1 Secure Moving Trucks
- 5.1.2 Pack Office Equipment

### 5.2 Execution of Move
- 5.2.1 Load Moving Trucks
- 5.2.2 Transport Office Equipment
- 5.2.3 Unload and Arrange Equipment

## Post-Move Activities
### 6.1 Data Reinstallation
- 6.1.1 Reinstall Data on New Network
- 6.1.2 Test Data Integrity

### 6.2 Ongoing Maintenance
- 6.2.1 Establish Maintenance Schedule
- 6.2.2 Perform Initial Maintenance Checks

### 6.3 Post-Move Support
- 6.3.1 Provide Support to Staff
- 6.3.2 Address Any Issues Arising Post-Move
